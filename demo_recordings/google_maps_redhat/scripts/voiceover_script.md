# Google Maps Demo - Finding the Closest Red Hat Office

## Demo Overview
A quick walkthrough showing how to use Google Maps to find the nearest Red Hat office and get directions.

**Total Duration:** ~1 minute
**Resolution:** 1680x1050

---

## Step 1: Introduction & Search (~15s)

**Narration:**
"Let's use Google Maps to find the closest Red Hat office. I'll start by searching for Red Hat offices in the search bar."

**Visual:** Google Maps homepage, typing search
**Actions:** Show Google Maps, type "Red Hat office" in search

---

## Step 2: Browse Results (~20s)

**Narration:**
"Google Maps shows us several Red Hat office locations. We can see the offices marked on the map with their addresses and ratings. Let's select one of the nearby offices to get more details."

**Visual:** Search results showing Red Hat offices
**Actions:** Show the list of results, click on one

---

## Step 3: Get Directions (~15s)

**Narration:**
"Now we can see the office details including the address, hours, and contact information. To get directions, simply click the Directions button and Google Maps will show you the best route to the Red Hat office."

**Visual:** Office details and directions
**Actions:** Show office info, click directions

---

## Step 4: Summary (~10s)

**Narration:**
"And that's how easy it is to find your nearest Red Hat office using Google Maps. You can choose driving, transit, or walking directions based on your preference."

**Visual:** Directions view with route options
**Actions:** Show route options
